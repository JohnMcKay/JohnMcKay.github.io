About Me

Hello! I'm John McKay, a research scientist for Amazon Alexa in Cambridge Mass. I work on entity resolution and natural language understanding. I have a PhD from Penn State where, under the watchful eye of my advisor Vishal Monga, I focused on synthetic aperture image formation and classification with neural nets. Before my time as a electrical engineer, I investigated topics in mathematical biology, which was the focus of my undergraduate research and masters degree. Simply put, there are few data science topics that I am not interested in. If you see anything on this website you'd like to know more about, feel free to contact me!
